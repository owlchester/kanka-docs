# Archived Entries

Archiving an entry is a way to declutter your campaign without permanently deleting content. An archived entry remains in your campaign but is hidden from most views.

## What happens when you archive an entry

- **Lists**: Archived entries no longer appear in category lists (e.g. Characters, Locations) by default.
- **Search**: Archived entries are excluded from the campaign search and all mention/autocomplete dropdowns.
- **Dashboard**: Archived entries do not appear in dashboard widgets (recent, random, etc.).
- **Maps**: Archived entries are not affected in map markers. They will still appear on maps.
- **Relations & Posts**: Existing relations and posts on an archived entry are preserved. The entry still exists; it's simply hidden.
- **Children**: Archiving a parent entry does **not** cascade to its children. Each entry must be archived individually.

## Viewing archived entries

To view archived entries, use the **Archived** filter in any category list. This will show only archived entries for that category.

## Archiving and unarchiving

Archiving and unarchiving is a toggle. You can do this from the entry's action menu (...), or via the API.

**Permissions**: Archiving requires the same permission as editing the entry (`update` permission). Any member with edit access to an entry can archive it.

## Archive vs. Delete

Archiving is non-destructive. All data, including relations, articles, and properties, is preserved and can be restored at any time by
unarchiving. Deleting an entry is permanent.
