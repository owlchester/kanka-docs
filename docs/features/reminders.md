# Reminders

Reminders are the term to link any entry in the campaign to one of its [calendars](/entries/calendars). An entry can be multiple times in a calendar, as well as being in multiple calendars.

Each entry has a **Reminders** subpage, which lists its various calendars.

## Special reminders

Entries such as characters, locations, families, and organisations have a special [reminder type](/advanced/age).
