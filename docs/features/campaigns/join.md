# Apply to an open campaign

Campaign Applications let you apply to join a campaign run by another GM. You fill in a short form about yourself and your preferences, and the GM reviews your application and either approves or rejects it.

---

## Finding a Campaign

Browse the public campaign listings to find campaigns that are open to applications.

![1](campaigns/img/join-1.png)

---

## Submitting an Application

When you find a campaign you'd like to join, click the **Apply** button on the campaign's dashboard. This opens the application form.

![2](campaigns/img/join-2.png)

---

### Character Concept

Describe the character you'd like to play. If you provide something here, it must be at least 10 characters long. This is your chance to pitch your character idea to the GM.

---

### Experience Level

Select the option that best describes your TTRPG experience:

| Option | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **New** | Little or no prior TTRPG experience |
| **Intermediate** | Some experience with one or more systems |
| **Veteran** | Extensive experience across many sessions or campaigns |

---

### Availability

**Available Days**

Check the days of the week you're generally available to play. This helps the GM understand your schedule.

**Session Start Time / End Time**

Enter your preferred session window (e.g. `05:00 PM` to `08:00 PM`).

**Timezone**

Select your UTC offset so the GM can compare your availability against the campaign's schedule.

---

### Preferences

**RP vs Combat**

A slider representing your preferred balance between roleplay and combat:

| Position | Label |
|---|---|
| Left | RP Heavy |
| Centre | Balanced _(default)_ |
| Right | Combat Focused |

**Tone**

A slider representing your preferred campaign tone:

| Position | Label |
|---|---|
| Left | Serious |
| Centre | Balanced _(default)_ |
| Right | Casual |

---

### External Link

A URL to any relevant external resource, such as a D&D Beyond character sheet or a character backstory document. Must be a valid URL starting with `https://`.

---

### Additional Notes _(optional)_

Any other information you'd like the GM to know. Maximum 255 characters.

---

## Submitting the Form

Click **Apply** to submit. You'll be redirected back to the campaign dashboard and the GM will be notified of your application.

![3](campaigns/img/join-3.png)

---

## Updating Your Application

If your availability or preferences change before the GM has made a decision, you can return to the campaign's apply page and edit your answers. Click **Update** to save the changes.

---

## Withdrawing Your Application

To cancel your application, return to the apply page and click the **Delete** button. Your application will be removed immediately.

---

## Application Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Your application has been submitted and is awaiting a GM decision. |
| **Approved** | The GM accepted your application. You are now a member of the campaign. |
| **Rejected** | The GM declined your application. You will receive an in-app notification. |

Once the GM reviews your application and either approves or rejects it you'll get a notification.

![4](campaigns/img/join-4.png)

Once an application is approved or rejected, it can no longer be edited or withdrawn.
